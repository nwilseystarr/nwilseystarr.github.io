About Me

I am a Bootcamp student at Northwestern Coding Bootcamp focusing on the full-stack MERN track. I have a degree in Political Science from DePaul University, and am the Executive-Director for the Model United Nations Development Organization which aims to challenge future leaders to think from a global perspective through immersive and experiential education. I am also the Chief Operating Officer at cvVentures where we work with and invest in startups and mature businesses in the medical/biotech, fintech, education, and real estate sectors in Asia and North America.

I have been “playing” with basic code since I was 14; however, through a project with cvVentures I really started to see it as more than just an idea. It was while working on a new fin-tech app in Hong Kong where I learned more about the ins and outs of programming. I am very excited to continue on my path of continued learning through Bootcamp and my career.
